01 / About

Bringing perception to crops

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Biosystems Engineering at Seoul National University (SNUPHEL Lab), expected to graduate in August 2026. My research sits at the intersection of computer vision, agricultural robotics, and plant science — building intelligent systems that perceive and quantify crops under real greenhouse and field conditions.

I am particularly interested in occlusion-robust perception, 3D plant reconstruction, and data-efficient learning via diffusion-based augmentation and self-supervised methods for precision agriculture. I enjoy tackling real-world agricultural challenges and am always open to collaboration.
